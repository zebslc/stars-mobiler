Hyper Expansion (HE)
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout" and three "Spore Cloud" mini-colony ships.
Traits:
Population growth rate is doubled (growthFactor: 2).
Maximum population is halved (maxPopulationOffset: -0.5).
Ships vanish if they run out of fuel in deep space.
Super Stealth (SS)
Starting Tech: Starts with Electronics at level 5.
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout" and one "Santa Maria" colony ship.
Traits:
Ships have 300 built-in cloaking units.
Freighters have free cargo cloaking.
Steals 50% of research from all fields when discovering another race's tech.
Minefields have a safe warp bonus of 1.
War Monger (WM)
Starting Tech: Starts with Weapons at level 6, and Energy and Propulsion at level 1.
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Santa Maria" colony ship, one "Armed Probe" fighter scout, and one "Gadfly" mini bomber.
Traits:
Techs in Construction, Electronics, and Planetary Defenses are 25% cheaper.
Discover enemy ship designs upon scanning them.
+65% bonus to attack during invasions.
+2 movement bonus for ships.
Claim Adjuster (CA)

Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout", one "Santa Maria" colony ship, and one "Change of Heart" mini-miner terraformer.
Traits:
Can instantly terraform planets.
10% chance to permanently terraform a planet when population reaches 100,000.
Inner Strength (IS)
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout" and one "Santa Maria" colony ship.
Traits:
Freighters grow at half the normal rate.

Techs in Terraforming are 40% cheaper.
Space Demolition (SD)
Starting Tech: Starts with Propulsion and Biotechnology at level 2.
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout", one "Santa Maria" colony ship, one "Little Hen" damage-focused mine layer, and one "Speed Turtle" speed-focused mine layer.
Traits:
Minefields act as scanners.
Minefield laying rate is halved while moving.
Minefield safe warp bonus is doubled.

Packet Physics (PP)
Starting Tech: Starts with Energy at level 4.
Starting Planets: Two planets.
A homeworld with 20,000 population, a Mass Driver, and a "Starbase".
A second planet with 10,000 population, a Mass Driver, and an "Accelerator Platform" on an "Orbital Fort" hull.
Traits:
Mineral packets are cheaper and more efficient.
Can detect all mineral packets and their destinations.
Packets have a chance to terraform planets.
Interstellar Traveler (IT)
Starting Tech: Starts with Propulsion and Construction at level 5.
Starting Planets: Two planets, both with Stargates.
A homeworld with 20,000 population and a "Starbase".
A second planet with 10,000 population and a "Porthole to Beyond" starbase on an "Orbital Fort" hull.
Starting Fleets: Includes scouts, a colonizer, a "Swashbuckler" Privateer, and a "Stalwart Defender" Destroyer.
Traits:
Stargating tech is 25% cheaper.
Mineral packets have higher costs and reduced reception.
Can gate cargo and detect other stargate-capable planets.
Alternate Reality (AR)
Starting Tech: Starts with Energy at level 1.
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: One "Long Range Scout" and one "Santa Maria" colony ship.
Traits:
Freighters have negative growth.
Can remote mine their own planets.
Starbases cost 20% less.
Innate mining, resources, and scanning abilities at 10% effectiveness.
Can live on starbases.
Minimum habitability is floored at 25.
Jack of All Trades (JOAT)
Starting Tech: Starts with all tech fields at level 3.
Starting Planets: One homeworld with 25,000 population, 10 mines, 10 factories, and 10 defenses. It includes a "Starbase" on a "Space Station" hull.
Starting Fleets: A diverse fleet including a scout, colonizer, freighter, miner, fighter scout, and destroyer.
Traits:
All technologies cost an additional level to research (Techs cost extra level 4).
Maximum population is increased by 20%.
Have built-in scanners with normal and penetrating capabilities.
